Crystal Collector!

Rules of the game are thus: you click any key to begin. The computerwill generate a number between 1 and 120. You must then click on a crystal in which the crystal's corresponding value will appear and begin to tally. to win, you must reach the random number without going over. If you tally up to the number you win, if you tally over that number, you lose. Good luck
